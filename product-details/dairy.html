Product Categories
Explore Our Dairy Offerings:
Milk

Savor Headon's farm-fresh milk sourced from local dairy farms dedicated to quality and animal welfare. Choose from creamy whole, light low-fat, or lactose-free options, perfect for drinks, cereal, or smoothies. Enjoy the wholesome goodness of Headon's milk.

Cheese

Discover Headon's artisanal cheeses, crafted from the finest milk by expert cheesemakers. From creamy brie to sharp cheddar, our selection offers a burst of flavor for any dish. Perfect for snacking, cooking, or entertaining, elevate your culinary creations with our premium cheeses.

Yogurts

Indulge in Headon's creamy yogurts, packed with probiotics and nutrients for gut health. From classic vanilla to exotic mango-passionfruit, our selection satisfies every craving. Enjoy them alone, with granola, or blended into smoothies and dips.

Milk

Experience the wholesome goodness of farm-fresh milk with Headon's dairy products. Sourced from local dairy farms known for their commitment to quality and animal welfare, our milk is rich in nutrients and flavor.

Whether you prefer whole milk for its creamy texture, low-fat milk for a lighter option, or lactose-free milk for those with dietary restrictions, our milk selection has something for everyone. Enjoy a refreshing glass of cold milk, pour it over your morning cereal, or use it as a base for smoothies and shakes.

Cheese

Elevate your culinary creations with Headon's artisanal cheeses. Made from the finest quality milk and crafted with care by expert cheesemakers, our cheese selection offers a variety of flavors and textures to suit every taste.

From creamy brie and tangy cheddar to sharp parmesan and crumbly feta, our cheeses are perfect for snacking, cooking, and entertaining. Add them to cheese boards, salads, sandwiches, or pasta dishes for a burst of flavor that will impress even the most discerning cheese lover.

Yogurts

Start your day off right with Headon's creamy and delicious yogurts. Made from fresh milk and live cultures, our yogurts are packed with probiotics and nutrients that support gut health and digestion.

Whether you prefer classic flavors like vanilla and strawberry or adventurous combinations like mango-passionfruit and coconut-chia, our yogurt selection has something to satisfy every craving. Enjoy them on their own, layer them with granola and fruit for a nutritious parfait, or use them as a creamy base for smoothies and dips.

Butter and Cream

Add richness and flavor to your favorite recipes with Headon's butter and cream products. Made from the finest quality milk and churned to perfection, our butter is creamy, rich, and perfect for spreading on toast, baking, or saut√©ing vegetables.

Our cream is smooth and luscious, ideal for whipping into fluffy peaks for desserts or adding richness to sauces and soups. Whether you're baking a batch of cookies, making a creamy pasta sauce, or whipping up a decadent dessert, Headon's butter and cream products are sure to elevate your culinary creations.

Plant-Based Alternatives

For those following a dairy-free lifestyle, Headon also offers a selection of plant-based alternatives.

From almond milk and coconut yogurt to cashew cheese and vegan butter, our plant-based products are made from wholesome ingredients and free from dairy, lactose, and animal products. Enjoy the same great taste and versatility of dairy without compromising on flavor or nutrition.

Fresh from farm to your home

At Headon, we're committed to providing you with dairy products that are not only delicious but also nutritious and sustainable. Explore our dairy selection and discover a world of freshness and flavor that will enhance your meals and nourish your body.
